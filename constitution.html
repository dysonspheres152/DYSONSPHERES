Preamble & Institutional Charter

We, the student body and administrative board of Congress High School, bind ourselves to the core values of discipline, legal compliance, and community progress. This charter provides legal authority for our institutional parameters.

Rules & General Conduct Standards

Every learner enrolled with Congress High School must maintain rigorous behavioral compliance. Prohibited metrics include substance abuse, property vandalism, theft, class absenteeism, and unauthorized items tracking parameters.
